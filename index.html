Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is a major Hindu celebration symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. It is celebrated by lighting oil lamps, decorating homes with rangolis, and bursting fireworks. Families worship Lord Ganesha and Goddess Lakshmi, seeking prosperity and happiness. The festival brings people together, fostering unity, joy, and kindness. Diwali is marked by the exchange of sweets, gifts, and well-wishes, creating an atmosphere of warmth and togetherness.
